About TLC

The Teaching Learning Centre (TLC) supports our educators through consultations, teaching resources, programs, seminars, workshops, and other events.

We work closely with departments to develop trainings specific to the needs of our teaching staff. We have designed tailored trainings and workshops around remote and hybrid teaching, inclusive teaching strategies, student engagement, specialized trainings, and more.

We aim to create an environment that values all perspectives, experiences, and contributions, where instructors and learners can engage and learn through open, multi-sided dialogue. We promote and practice evidence-based teaching-learning strategies.
